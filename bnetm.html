Battle.net Mobile

Battle.net is Blizzard’s online gaming, social, distribution and rights management platform. Originally lanched in 1996, Battle.net serves as a primary touch point for every Blizzard gaming franchise. In 2017 Battle.net came to mobile for the first time.

2017 (v0.1 - 1.0)

In late 2016 I embed in the Battle.net organization as part of a cross-departmental strike team tasked with defining, designing, building and launching the platform’s first mobile client in its 20 year history. After we successfully shipped our MVP at BlizzCon 2017 I transitioned off of the team.

2019 Redesign (v1.5 and Beyond)

In early 2019, following an internal reorg, I joined the newly formed Battle.net mobile team which took sole ownership of the product. Currently myself and another designer are working to developer a streamlined design system to meet accessibility standards and modernize UI patterns. I am also actively working with tech and PM disciplines to define vision and strategy for the app moving forward with the mandate to expand the product's scope beyond the existing social functionality.
